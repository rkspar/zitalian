About Zuckerello's

The Story

The concept of Zuckerello's Italian Restaurant began in November of 1989 in the mind of Adam Zucker. His desire was to operate a neighborhood restaurant and bar. It was important to him that Zuckerello's be a place where locals could come and dine in a comfortable and relaxing atmosphere. After 12 years, in 2001, Eli Biton joined Adam in this endeavor, and pursued the same envision for the concept of Zuckerello's Italian Restaurant.

THROUGH BOTH OF THEIR EFFORTS AND THEIR WONDERFUL STAFF, ZUCKERELLO'S HAS BECOME ONE OF THE MOST POPULAR RESTAURANTS IN FT. LAUDERDALE. A FEW MEMBERS OF THE STAFF HAVE BEEN WORKING WITH ZUCKERELLO'S FOR THE PAST 26 YEARS.

RECENTLY, THE RESTAURANT OPENED FOR dinner AND NOW THE LOCALS CAN ENJOY ZUCKERELLO'S FINEST DURING dinner TIME AND DINNER TIME!

Consistently superb, they are definitely doing something right. In the 6 years I've lived in Fort Lauderdale, dining there twice a month, we have yet to have a disappointing meal. Food is always good, service always outstanding - no wonder they are filled to capacity every night. kudos to you!!!!

-- Ana Sevilla
